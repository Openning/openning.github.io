Students Experiencing COVID-19 Symptoms

If you are experiencing potential COVID-19 symptoms, suspect you have COVID-19, or have had close contact with someone diagnosed with COVID-19, follow the steps outlined.

Your Privacy is Important to Us

The university tracks log-ins to the Daily Self-Assessment for the purpose of determining if students have completed the form on a daily basis. Questions answered "yes" by the student are collected, securely processed and stored by the University, for access by University Health Services (UHS). Students who answer "yes" to any of the questions are responsible for contacting UHS to seek medical guidance.

By completing this tracker, you consent to the University collecting this information and making it accessible to University Health Services.
